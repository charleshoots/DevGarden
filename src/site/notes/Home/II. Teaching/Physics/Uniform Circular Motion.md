# Definition 
Uniform circular motion is an object rotating at with constant [[Speed\|linear speed.]] An object in uniform circular motion will also have constant [[Home/II. Teaching/Physics/Angular Speed\|angular speed]]. The rotation around a circle is caused by the [[Home/II. Teaching/Physics/Centripetal Force\|centripetal force]] and [[Home/II. Teaching/Physics/Centripetal Acceleration\|centripetal acceleration]], which are always perpendicular to [[Velocity\|linear velocity vector]].
