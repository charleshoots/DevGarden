# Definition 
Speed is a [[Scalar & Vectors\|scalar]] quantity that just tells you how fast you are moving. It's always positive. Mathematically, it's the [distance](Distance) over time.

$$
\text{Speed}=\frac{\text{Distance}}{\text{Time}}
$$
