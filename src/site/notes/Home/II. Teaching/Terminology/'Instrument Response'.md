Instrument Response

Sunday, January 05, 2014

6:00 PM

instrument response: a set of parameters built into the [Physics](charleshoots.net/II.%20Teaching/Physics) of the recording instrument. These are well known and can be removed from a seismogram by deconvolution of the transfer function and the seismic signal to recover the source derived signals.
