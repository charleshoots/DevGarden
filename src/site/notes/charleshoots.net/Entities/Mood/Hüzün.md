_Hüzün_, as described by Orhan Pamuk in [Istanbul](../../Reading/Have%20Read/Istanbul%20by%20Orhan%20Pamuk.md), is melancholia that one feels for things lost or time past. This is an all-pervading mood of post-Ottoman Istanbul.
