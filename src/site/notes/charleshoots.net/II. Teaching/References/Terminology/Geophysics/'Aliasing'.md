Aliasing

Sunday, January 05, 2014

5:59 PM

aliasing: a phenomena where frequencies are overlapping each other, and the the individual contributions from those frequencies are unable to be deciphered. This is avoided by decreasing the sampling interval such that it is higher than the highest angular frequency content of the signal (seeNyquist frequency).
