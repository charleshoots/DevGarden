tsunami magnitude (Mt)

Sunday, January 05, 2014

6:01 PM

A number used to compare sizes of tsunamis generated by different earthquakes and calculated from the logarithm of the maximum amplitude of the tsunami wave measured by a tide gauge distant from the tsunami source.
