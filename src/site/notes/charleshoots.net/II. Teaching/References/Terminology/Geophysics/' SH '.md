SH

Sunday, January 05, 2014

6:00 PM

A shear wave that is polarized such that it's particle motion and direction of propogation occur in a horizontal plane parallel to the surface. A component of an S-wave having displacements parallel to the surface. Part of the S wave that helps form Love waves.
